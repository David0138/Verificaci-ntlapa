# Deployment Instructions for Railway

## Overview
This document provides step-by-step instructions for deploying the Verificaci-ntlapa application on Railway.

## Prerequisites
- A Railway account
- Access to the Verificaci-ntlapa repository

## Steps for Deployment

1. **Create a New Project**  
   - Log in to your Railway account.
   - Click on the 'New Project' button.

2. **Connect Your Repository**  
   - Select 'Deploy from GitHub'.
   - Authorize Railway to access your GitHub account.
   - Search for the `David0138/Verificaci-ntlapa` repository and connect it.

3. **Configure Environment Variables**  
   - Go to the 'Settings' tab of your project.
   - Add any necessary environment variables needed for your application to run.

4. **Deploy the Application**  
   - Click on the 'Deploy' button.  
   - Wait for the deployment process to complete. You can view the logs for any errors or issues.

5. **Access Your Application**  
   - Once deployed, you will receive a URL to access your application.  
   - Test the app to ensure it works as expected.

## Additional Information
- For more details, refer to the [Railway documentation](https://railway.app/docs).

## Troubleshooting
- If you encounter issues, check the logs for errors. You may need to adjust your configuration settings.
